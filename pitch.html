UNMUTE

A meeting voice proxy that gives everyone an equal voice in the room.

Mountain Madness 2026

The Problem

  • Not everyone finds it easy to jump into fast-moving conversations.
  • People with social anxiety may struggle to interject when the pressure is high.
  • Non-native speakers often need a moment to compose their thoughts in a second language.
  • Individuals with stutters or other speech differences want to participate without pressure.
  • Many people simply think better in writing than on the spot.

Our Solution

  • UNMUTE doesn't replace your voice; it gives it back to you.
  • You type your thought into the web UI, and your response is prepared in the background while the conversation continues.
  • It listens to your call and detects when you are being invited to speak.
  • It delivers your thoughts at exactly the right moment—clearly, confidently, and on time.

Key Features

  • Live Transcript: Real-time speech-to-text ensures you always know what is being said.
  • Trigger Words: Auto-fires your queued response when a cue phrase—like "any questions" or your name—is detected mid-speech.
  • Cancellation Delay: A visible 1-second countdown gives you a moment to press Stop before your response plays.
  • Manual Speak: Take control and press "Speak" to jump in manually whenever it feels right.

Under The Hood

  • Audio Routing: Utilizes BlackHole 2ch to simultaneously capture meeting audio and route output.
  • Backend Engine: Built using Python, Flask, and Flask-SocketIO for asynchronous event handling.
  • Speech-to-Text: Employs the ElevenLabs Scribe v2 model over websockets for real-time live transcription.
  • Text-to-Speech: Uses the ElevenLabs Flash v2.5 model to quickly generate high-quality audio responses.
